# Job-Search Copy — Zack Wilde

Reusable templates for applications and outreach. Replace the **[BRACKETED]**
parts per role. Stay honest — don't add numbers you can't back up, and tailor
the one specific "why you" sentence for each company (it beats a generic letter
every time). Keep cover letters under ~300 words; hiring managers skim.

## Cover letter (template)

Dear [HIRING MANAGER / TEAM],

I'm writing to apply for the [ROLE] at [COMPANY]. [ONE SPECIFIC SENTENCE ABOUT WHY THIS COMPANY — a product you admire, their mission, the stack, the problem they solve.] I'm a full-stack developer who builds web and mobile products end to end, and it looks like a strong fit for what you're building.

Over the past year I've designed, built, and shipped several full-stack products on my own:

- **Daily 60** — an Expo mobile app with a server-side AI backend. The Claude API key stays behind an Express/TypeScript API with input validation, quota checks, and CI, so the app never ships secrets or trusts raw model output.
- **FinTrack** — a finance dashboard built on a 7-table Supabase schema with 9 row-level-security policies that isolate each business's data, plus a CSV import-and-review workflow.
- **Kakebo Shop** — a live Shopify storefront, and a client website I built and delivered for a local service business.

Each one is live with a working demo at zackwilde-dev.netlify.app, so you can see the actual product rather than take my word for it.

What I enjoy is owning a feature from data model to deployment and finishing the details that make it usable — clear screens, tests, CI, and clean handoffs. I'm comfortable across [TAILOR: React/TypeScript, Node/Express, Postgres/Supabase] and I pick up new tools quickly. [OPTIONAL: one sentence connecting your experience to something in the job description.]

I'd welcome the chance to talk about how I can contribute to [COMPANY]. My resume and live projects are at zackwilde-dev.netlify.app, and I'm reachable any time at zackwildebusiness@gmail.com.

Thank you for your time.

Best,
Zack Wilde
zackwildebusiness@gmail.com · zackwilde-dev.netlify.app · github.com/zackwildebusiness-glitch
